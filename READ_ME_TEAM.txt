Where to see Team & Invite Link
=================================
1) Open the site Home — the nav now has **Team** between Assets and Deposit.
2) Click **Team** — you'll see your Invite Code and Invite Link boxes.
   - If you don't have a code yet, the page auto-generates and saves one (tmInviteCode).
3) Your invite link format:
   https://<your-domain>/index.html?ref=<YOURCODE>&u2=<YOUR_UPLINE_CODE_optional>
